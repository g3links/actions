What is G3 Links: Actions

In a nut-shell, Actions allows individuals and small businesses to stream their daily tasks via the use of a collection of micro-applications. Micro-applications allow the creation of highly focused, task-based functionality that lets users get in, interact and get out with maximum efficiency.

At G3 Links we believe everyone should have the opportunity to be part of a more connected world. Create your own projects and applications for free, forever, no strings attached. G3 Links does not collects or shares any information; terms and conditions are bounded to those providers.

For more information you can contact us at: info@g3links.com


Why should I use G3 Links: Actions

  • Security: Select a trusted provider or be part of a private network to start using the applications.
  • Private: Create projects for both personal and team use, at your privacy wishes.
  • Collaboration: Invite others members to join and collaborate with actions, at the controlled privacy.
  • Public: Make projects public, at anytime, and let others interact with your actions.
  • Networking: Join public projects and interact (promote, sell, collaborate to name a few).
  • Team: This tools allow members to collaborate on common goals.
  • Free: You're in control, all the time, you can sign-in and sign-out at anytime you wish.
